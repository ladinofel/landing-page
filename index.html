About Us
INMUNOTEK COLOMBIA is a pharmaceutical laboratory located in the city of Santiago de Cali, Colombia. The company commercializes products in the field of allergy and immunology for the diagnosis and treatment of human pathologies. Operations began in Colombia in March 2013. Since its creation, our goal has been to offer top quality, innovative and competitive products useful for the health professional, and safe and effective for the patients.
